Cesium Endpoint Elevation Adjustment Utility

Polylines and Elevation

Put simply, when a polyline does not use 'clamp to ground' the starting and ending points of the polyline rarely terminate at the ground. Consequently, before a QSO map is published on the blog, we do an extra step to at least line up the line with the ground on the transmit side. Transmit station higher than ground at station location.

This required adjustment led to another LLM prototyped tool. The tool adjusts the elevation of the starting point, (the tranmsit sation), of a radio conatct skip map.
